ORCID ID creation and linking

University faculty, staff and students who wish to get a new ORCID identifier should use the “Create My ORCID” option.

If you already have an ORCID record, please click “Provide Existing ORCID” to allow us to associate your campus id with your ORCID identifier.

Please note that publishers have begun asking for ORCID identifiers during the manuscript submission process, and there are several benefits to getting an ORCID iD, which will follow you throughout your scholarly career.
More information available at orcid.org
